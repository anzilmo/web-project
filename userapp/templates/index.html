PRODUCT DETAILSA2 WHITE

CERRANT A2 WHITE is Portland cement based adhesive appropriate for suitable mainly for tiles and stone tiles on exterior wall substrate like plaster or concrete it is suitable for tiles like ceramic, claytiles, Basalt tiles, vitrified tiles, glass mosaic tiles, porcelain tiles and all natural stone tiles.

COVERAGE

The extent of coverage is contingent upon the surface's condition, texture, and the technique of application. When applied with a thin-bed trowel at a thickness of 3-4mm, the coverage ranges approximately from 3-4 Kg/M2. To ensure optimal coverage, it is imperative that all surfaces are meticulously cleaned, smooth, vertically aligned, level, and devoid of any defects or irregularities.

PRODUCT DETAILSA3 GREY

CERRANT A3 GREY is Portland cement based adhesive appropriate for most small and medium sized clay and ceramic tiles and can be applied to cement-based substrates such as cement plaster, cement concrete, and cement screed. It should only be used for interior dry applications, such as interior wall and floor tiling.

COVERAGE

The extent of coverage is contingent upon the surface's condition, texture, and the technique of application. When applied with a thin-bed trowel at a thickness of 3-4mm, the coverage ranges approximately from 3-4 Kg/M2. To ensure optimal coverage, it is imperative that all surfaces are meticulously cleaned, smooth, vertically aligned, level, and devoid of any defects or irregularities.

PRODUCT DETAILSA2 GREY

CERRANT A2 GREY is Portland cement based adhesive appropriate for most vitrified or fully vitrified tiles, glass mosaic tiles, all stones, dense and large dimensions where background and location is more demand- Ing like wet areas, submerged areas etc. this type of adhesive recom- mended for both interior floor and wall applications and for exterior floor applications.

COVERAGE

This will depend on the nature and lateness of the surface and the method of application but at 3-4mm thickness it is approx 3-4 Kg/M2 when using a thin-bed trowel. For good coverage, it is important that all surfaces should be clean, smooth, plumb, level and free from defects & undulations.
